After checking out the project:

Open PHPMyAdmin by right clicking on Wamp icon at the right bottom of your screen
Now click on "Import" tab
Click on "Choose file"
Now traverse to the C:/wamp/www/Zav-DBRB directory
Select dbrb.sql and import
Now your database is ready

Go to  C:/wamp/www/Zav-DBRB/app/config/database.php
In this file change database name to "dbrb"
You are good to go!
